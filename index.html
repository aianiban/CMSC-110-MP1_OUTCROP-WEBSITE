ABOUT

  • The official student publication in the University of the Philippines Baguio.
  • A vehicle for the ventilation, synthesis, and expression of ideas and needs of the entire studentry encompassing all aspects of human endeavor both within and outside the limits of the academic center.
  • It shall serve as the mouthpiece of all sectors of the academic community. It shall be a medium by which the current issues of greatest concern and relevance to the students can be dealt with.
  • Outcrop shall endeavor to courageously and responsibly exercise the right of the students to free expression.
